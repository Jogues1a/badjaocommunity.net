Project Summary

The problem that our project aims to solve is the lack of food. It is important to address and solve this problem because food insecurity makes people prone to illnesses and a weaker immune system as they are unable to take in enough nutrients and vitamins to support their body, they may develop certain health issues like high blood pressure, heart disease, diabetes, malnutrition etc. which can be caused by hunger, and children are especially vulnerable to these dire economic circumstances which puts them at greater risk. Finding a solution to this problem will not only help them attain their basic needs like food but they will also have a more reliable and stable source of income which then helps them earn enough money for their basic necessities and to provide for their families. And also our project aims to educate the Badjao Community to survive in daily lives while dealing with other problems. We encourage them to learn how to properly grow and sustain vegetable food in times of shortage problems. Keeping these things in mind will now help us come up with better solutions that would help us solve the said issue more effectively especially at our young age, which is how we came up with the idea of donating. Given that their business of selling items such as, clothes, shoes, accessories, and toys is one of their main sources of income, we have agreed that donating our pre-loved items that are still in good condition would be the best solution that we could offer to them as donating our pre-loved items will provide them with more things to sell for their business and will help them earn more money. In addition, we will also be boosting their business through social media by posting about their community, needs, and business because this will help us educate people about the Badjao community and encourage others to reach out, help, and support the community. Also encourage other people to donate vegetable seeds to help the Badjao community in planting healthy foods. And it helps them to survive in case no one can’t donate because other people have their own problems too like financial problems. With that being said, we believe that our proposed solution will help the Badjao community have a stable source of income and attain their basic necessities.

Problem Statement

The shortage of basic necessities, including food, is an issue in the Badjao Community. They are unable to purchase suitable foods, such as wholesome and nutritious foods, due to their inconsistent source of income and poverty. A Badjao uses a variety of methods to support themselves in the city, including fishing and hawking second-hand clothing on the streets. Due to their severe discrimination and lack of access to wholesome, nutritional meals for their growth, children are also impacted. The Badjaos struggled as was to be expected during a period when the Coronavirus had partially compelled people to stay at home. Since the city was in lockdown, they were all having trouble finding work and making money. They have few options for surviving, and they are powerless. We, therefore, decide what the best way is to assist them in surviving. We considered donating our pre-loved items which are still in good condition and encouraging others to do the same. Giving to the organizations that represent the cause you care about benefits both the charities and you personally. Millions of people regularly donate to charities to support issues they care about and for the benefits it brings to their own lives.
Problem Description

The problem of the Badjao community is the lack of basic needs, most especially, food. Changes in the law have banned fishing in the areas where the Badjao have been fishing for centuries, because of this, they struggle to find food because this is one of their only sources of food. This also affects them financially as this is one of their sources of income, without access to fishing, they won’t have fish to sell and they continue to struggle in poverty and hunger. Because they lack food, they become prone to certain illnesses such as high blood pressure, heart disease, diabetes, malnutrition, etc. which can be caused by hunger, this is because they are not able to take in enough nutrients and vitamins, hence a weaker immune system, making them prone to these illnesses. We want the Badjao community to experience fair treatment without injustice, corruption, discrimination, and oppression. As much as possible, we want to help them to live a comfortable life, especially the children of the community as they are more vulnerable to these dire economic circumstances. The purpose or improvement that we wish to accomplish is to help them gain access to more food since it is one of the most essential needs of our body and it is something they struggle to find. We want to see them live a long, happy, and healthy life. On top of this, we want to give them hope that their life will eventually get better as long as we all work together and have that willingness and passion to help their community.

✨Mission✨


The organization's advocacy includes developing a program for both adults and children

as well as taking part in environmental initiatives like restoring and protecting the coastline.

The Badjao Community is famous for their vibrant sails, which are also renowned for their artistically-woven.

Particularly for preschoolers, the Badjao community offers events and activities.

In fact, they collaborate with their local government on one of their programs that is centered on family and health.

The Badjao community association was founded with the intention of setting an example for the residents of the Badjao community.

Initially, the association had about 35 families. The community now numbers more than 500 people.

✨History✨



Some Bajaos have been seen around Davaos busy streets asking for food from others just to live.

Some Bajao have started to make a community along the shore and have initiated

the Bajao Association Community of Matina Aplaya Inc. The association was established

with a purpose of being a model for the Bajao community. It was all started with the

guidance of Pastor Felicisimo “Jun” Morales more than 8 years ago, he and his wife

lived on Isla Verde with the Bajao which helped establish the institution.

✨Organizer✨


The BAMAI (Badjau of Matina Aplaya Incorporation), which is led by Goldam Turani,

is the group that established the institution, Additionally, they implement literacy

programs for adults and preschoolers. In collaboration with the local government,

they also prioritize health and family planning. The community also conducts

and supports training for the development and preservation of their cultural heritage.

Finally, they engage in environmental restoration and prevention along the coastline.

✨Beneficiaries✨


The institution of the Badjao community in Matina Aplaya inhabits a total of

700 individuals from 174 families. An estimated 99 children are enrolled in Matina Aplaya

Elementary Schooling. The main source of income for the people in the Badjao community is

fishing and ambulant vending. The environment of the place where the people of

the Badjao community live is that there's a lot of waste not being properly disposed of.

✨Benefactors✨

The association started with the guidance of Pastor Felicisimo “Jun” Morales.

Together with his wife, they lived with the Badjao in Isla Verde for more than 8 years.

In past interventions, Ateneo has also provided waste segregation materials to help maintain

the cleanliness of the community since there are a lot of wastes not being properly disposed of.

This kind of intervention will provide the community with a better and healthier environment.
