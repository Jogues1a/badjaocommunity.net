Partner Institution Profile Community Project

Our gallery page contains our community feature video
which has different kinds of information about our partner community. The video was created in
hopes that it would educate others about the cause, context, and needs of the Badjao community
and inspire others to participate in bringing change in order to provide the community with a
better life. This page also contains our artworks in which we made use of the different techniques
taught to us in our Art class. In our ET1, we created an artwork which represents being human with
all sorts of different emotions and interpretations displayed. Lastly, our ET2 represents
prehistoric art which shows what art was like back then and what life was like back when history
was not yet recorded.
